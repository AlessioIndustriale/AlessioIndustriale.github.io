Industrial Symbiosis Dictionary

The Industrial Symbiosis Dictionary born with the aim of making it easier to adopt symbiotic synergies between companies documenting links already present into the industrial world

Insert at least one input into the form to filter the dataset and get the records of interest

The technical replacement coefficient represents the percentage that a unit of secondary material is able to replace respect to a unit of virgin material

Within the "how to use the waste" field you can find either the materials directly replaced by the waste or the production processes in which it is used, the presence of one of the two clearly depends on the information present in the article examined
